# Guide: Linear vs radial gradients

Use a linear gradient when you want direction — a diagonal hero, a top-to-bottom fade. Use radial when you want a glow or spotlight from a point. Keep the two stops close in hue for a subtle, modern look, or far apart for a bold one. Gradients layer well over photos as readability overlays.
